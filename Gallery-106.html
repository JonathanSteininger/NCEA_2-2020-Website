SCP-106

Object Class: Keter

SCP-106 is an old man that looks like a decayed black body consisting of a black mold and decayed flesh.

SCP-106 decays anything he touches at a rapid rate, he can travel through walls and always leaves a decayed mark behing.

Being touched by SCP-106 can induce major irritation, and SCP-106 can teleport anyone he touches into his own pocket dimension where he slowly decays the victim.

SCP-106 feeds of other peoples suffering and will play with his meal in horrific ways.

If you encounter this SCP refrain from getting close. to escape SCP-106 you can outrun him as he is usually very slow due to his decay.
