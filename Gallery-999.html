SCP-999

Object Class: safe

SCP-999 can cure any depression. Coming in contact with SCP-999 can cause a sensation of tickling and happiness.

After many tests including going against SCP-682 have concluded with the effected target feeling happy around SCP-999.

If you come in contact with SCP-999 dont be afraid as its a non hostil SCP with positive effects.
